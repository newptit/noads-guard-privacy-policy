NoAds Guard™ - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy: July 7, 2022

Our Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect from users and how we use it. It is important for you to have a complete understanding of your interaction with us. By installing our extension or visiting our website, you agree to the practices listed in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact our Support service.

What type of information does NoAds Guard™ collect?

NoAds Guard™ only stores necessary anonymized user data on its servers for the extension's functionality. Basic anonymized data, such as IP address, GEO location, and extension version number, is tracked to optimize the extension's functionality and overall user experience. All data is encrypted and anonymized at all times. Furthermore, we use the industry standard third-party service, Google Analytics, to collect certain information about our visitors to better understand their experience. This information includes session length and frequency, return rate, technical information such as browser type, version, screen resolution, and more. We do not collect any device identifier or personal information, and IP addresses are anonymized before transmission. All collected information is highly encrypted.

What does NoAds Guard™ do with the information collected?

We analyze the combined usage statistics to identify areas where NoAds Guard™ can be improved, monitor overall speed and error rates. We examine the supported devices, browsers, and resolutions. We also consider language and other locale information to determine the most useful localizations for our users and enhance the extension's functionality in different regions.

Usage

The above-mentioned data will be used to improve the user experience of NoAds Guard™ in general. The data is aggregated and anonymized when used for internal operations. This happens only for the purpose of improving the functionality of NoAds Guard™ directly. At all times, NoAds Guard™ acts in accordance to privacy and other legal requirements. We never share data with any third party. The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Permissions

NoAds Guard™ requires a certain amount of permissions, so you can enjoy its full potential. For security reasons we use the narrowest permissions available to still provide you the full functionality. These permissions are

  • tabs - to determine, if there is an ad on the current tab
  • webRequest & blocking - to provide the core functionality
  • storage & unlimitedStorage - to save settings
  • http://*/* & https://*/* - to provide the core functionality for videos on all websites

Duration

Data may be kept indefinitely.

Commerce

Your personal identifiable data is never bartered or sold for commercial gain.

Advertising

Your personal data is never given or sold to advertisers or otherwise used for marketing purposes.

Law Enforcement

Data is given to law enforcement only when legal process is followed.
