NoAds Guard™ - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy: July 7, 2022

This page lists the types of information that we collect from our users and shows what exactly we use that information for. We want to make sure you are aware of it so that you always see the whole picture of your interaction with us. Please read this Privacy Policy in full as by installing our extension or visiting our website you express your acceptance of the practices we list here. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, don’t hesitate to contact our Support service.

What type of information does NoAds Guard™ collect?

NoAds Guard™ does only store anonymized user data on its own servers that is necessary for the functionality of the extension. In order to optimize the functionality of NoAds Guard™ and overall user experience, basic anonymized data is tracked in the form of IP address and GEO Location and extension version number (all data will remain anonymized and encrypted at all times). In addition, we utilize the industry standard third party Google Analytics service to collect certain information about our visitors to better understand their experience. This information include the users' time spent on our site, session length and frequency, return rate, technical information such as browser type, version, screen resolution and more. We don't collect any device identifier or personal information, IP addresses are anonymized before transmitted. Every information that is collected is highly encrypted.

What does NoAds Guard™ do with the information collected?

We look at the aggregated usage statistics to better understand where NoAds Guard™ needs improving, monitor overall speed issues and error rates. We look at what devices, browsers and resolutions to support. We consider language and other locale information to determine what possible localizations would be most useful to our users and to improve the extension functionality in different geographic.

Usage

The above-mentioned data will be used to improve the user experience of NoAds Guard™ in general. The data is aggregated and anonymized when used for internal operations. This happens only for the purpose of improving the functionality of NoAds Guard™ directly. At all times, NoAds Guard™ acts in accordance to privacy and other legal requirements. We never share data with any third party. The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Permissions

NoAds Guard™ requires a certain amount of permissions, so you can enjoy its full potential. For security reasons we use the narrowest permissions available to still provide you the full functionality. These permissions are

  • tabs - to determine, if there is an ad on the current tab
  • webRequest & blocking - to provide the core functionality
  • storage & unlimitedStorage - to save settings
  • http://*/* & https://*/* - to provide the core functionality for videos on all websites

Duration

Data may be kept indefinitely.

Commerce

Your personal identifiable data is never bartered or sold for commercial gain.

Advertising

Your personal data is never given or sold to advertisers or otherwise used for marketing purposes.

Law Enforcement

Data is given to law enforcement only when legal process is followed.
