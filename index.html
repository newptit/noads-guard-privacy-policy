NoAds BlueGuard™ - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy: July 8, 2022

Our Privacy Policy clearly explains the data we gather from our users and how we use it. It is crucial for you to have a comprehensive understanding of your interaction with our platform. By installing our extension or accessing our website, you acknowledge and consent to the practices mentioned in this policy. For any inquiries or concerns, please feel free to reach out to our Support team.

What type of information does NoAds BlueGuard™ collect?

NoAds BlueGuard™ ensures the protection of your privacy by only storing necessary anonymized user data on its servers for the extension's functionality. The collected data, which includes basic anonymized information such as IP address, GEO location, and extension version number, is used to optimize the extension's functionality and overall user experience. All data is encrypted and anonymized at all times to guarantee your privacy. Moreover, we utilize the industry-standard third-party service, Google Analytics, to gather certain information about our visitors, including session length and frequency, return rate, technical information such as browser type, version, screen resolution, and more. We do not collect any personal information or device identifier, and IP addresses are anonymized before transmission. Rest assured that all collected information is highly encrypted for your peace of mind.

What does NoAds BlueGuard™ do with the information collected?

We evaluate aggregated usage data to pinpoint opportunities for enhancing NoAds BlueGuard™, as well as to keep track of general speed and error rates. We assess the supported devices, browsers, and resolutions. We also take into account language and other locale details to determine the most valuable localizations for our users and enhance the extension's functionality in different regions.

Usage

To enhance the overall user experience of NoAds BlueGuard™, the data mentioned above is analyzed and used for internal operations. The data is anonymized and aggregated before being used to improve the functionality of the extension. We prioritize user privacy and strictly adhere to all legal requirements. NoAds BlueGuard™ never shares any data with third parties. We ensure that the use of information received from Google APIs follows the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, which includes adhering to the Limited Use requirements.

Permissions

NoAds BlueGuard™ requires a certain amount of permissions, so you can enjoy its full potential. For security reasons we use the narrowest permissions available to still provide you the full functionality. These permissions are:

  • "alarms": needed to schedule periodic tasks for the extension.
  • "scripting": needed to interact with web pages and modify their content.
  • "storage" and "unlimitedStorage": needed to store and retrieve user settings and data.
  • "webNavigation": needed to track and analyze user navigation patterns for better functionality and user experience.
  • "declarativeNetRequest": needed to block unwanted content and ads on web pages.
  • "cookies": needed to access and modify browser cookies for better functionality and user experience.
  • "host_permission: all_urls": needed to allow the extension to access and interact with all URLs visited by the user, enabling it to perform its intended function.
